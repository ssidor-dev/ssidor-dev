DeJarnette Sanitarium

The DeJarnette Sanitarium was founded by Dr. Joseph DeJarnette in 1932. Known among his colleagues as "Sterilization DeJarnette". Dr. DeJarnette performed forced sterilizations on patients he deemed "unfit" to breed. In 1975 the Sanitariumcame under control of the state and was transformed into a children's hospital, renamed the DeJarnette Center for Human Development. In 1996 the hospital was abandoned, the relocated center was renamed the Commonwealth Center for Children and Adolescents due to DeJarnette's association with eugenics. The state of Virginia has publicly apologized for the sterilization program, and offered a reparations settlement to its victims.
