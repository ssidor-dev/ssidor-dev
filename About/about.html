Hi! I'm Stephen Sidor and I'm a photographer based in Newport News Virginia. I enjoy photographing abandoned places, historic cemeteries, and other interesting things. I started taking an interest in photography about 6 years ago. Exploring abandoned places is one of my favorite activities, I enjoy the stillness while walking through places forgotten by time and scociety.

I currently shoot with a Nikon D7200 and use both a 50mm and 24mm lens with the occasional red filter.
